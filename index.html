Greetings!

Welcome to my corner of the web! I’m Caroline, and I love to make things – like this website that I coded from scratch! I earned my BA’s in Theater and English from Cedar Crest College before being awarded my MFA (Costume Design and Technology) from Carnegie Mellon University. Over the years, I have been responsible for costuming hundreds of actors and actresses for various plays, musicals, and dance shows from coast to coast. As exciting as it is working with this range of performers and artists, I’m ready for new challenges in the tech world! Building products from scratch using code feel s a little like magic to me, and I love playing the part of magician!

In my spare time, I'm loving learning how to code -- so far I know HTML and CSS (as you can clearly see!), a little bit of JavaScript, and the basics of Python. I just learned about responsive web design, so I've just redesigned to make sure this site looks decent on all devices. I'm still picking up the finer points of that though!

When I'm not hammering away on my laptop, I enjoy creating a wide variety of crafts (chainmaile, knitting, drawing and painting), run an Etsy shop ( Magiciansassistant.Etsy.com), build costumes for myself and others (check out my costume portfolio page for examples), read anything I can get my hands on, and am constantly preparing for upcoming craft shows. My husband, a profession magician, also keeps me busy figuring out how to make various props for stage magic.
